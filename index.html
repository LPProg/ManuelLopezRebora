About me

Hi! I’m Manuel López Rébora, a passionate developer from Málaga, born in 1993. Since I was a child, I’ve been fascinated by video games and programming, which led me to pursue a career in software development. This journey brought me to specialize in cross-platform app development, where I continue to grow and learn every day. In addition to my work as a developer, I am also a multi-adventure instructor, which allows me to bring creativity and problem-solving skills into everything I do. I’m always updating my knowledge and skills, staying on top of the latest technologies and trends in the industry. I’m excited about the future and the opportunities to create innovative and meaningful digital experiences!
